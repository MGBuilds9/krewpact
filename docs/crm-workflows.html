Stage Definitions

Stage Meaning Moved By
New Just entered the system. No outreach yet. auto on creation
Contacted Rep has made first contact (email, call, LinkedIn). manual
Qualified Confirmed budget, timeline, decision-maker access. manual
Proposal Estimate/quote sent. Waiting on client response. manual
Negotiation Active back-and-forth on pricing, scope, or terms. manual
Nurture Not ready now, but worth keeping warm. manual
Won Deal closed. Lead converts to Account + Opportunity. manual Convert action
Lost Dead — no deal. Reason required. manual

Automated Background Layer

01 Enrichment Apollo → Clearbit → LinkedIn → Google waterfall fills company data
02 Scoring Rules engine: fit + intent + engagement → score 0–100
03 Account Match Compare against existing customers. ≥80% = flagged
04 Suppression Existing customers blocked from outreach sequences
05 Auto-Enroll High-scoring leads enter outreach sequences
06 Sequences Multi-step email/call cadence via QStash workers
07 AI Insights Gemini Flash generates next-best-action suggestions

05 Automated vs Manual

The automated layer handles enrichment, scoring, matching, and sequence sends. The rep focuses on human judgment: qualifying leads, composing custom outreach, advancing the pipeline, and closing deals.

Automated (no rep action)
  • Lead scoring & re-scoring
  • Data enrichment (Apollo waterfall)
  • Account matching & suppression
  • Sequence email sends
  • SLA overdue flagging
  • AI insight generation
  • Bid board imports (MERX)
  • Smoke test & health monitoring
Manual (rep must act)
  • Stage transitions
  • Custom email composition
  • Activity logging (calls, meetings)
  • Enrollment approval/rejection
  • Pipeline stage advancement
  • Estimate creation
  • Lead conversion (Won)
  • Lost reason documentation
