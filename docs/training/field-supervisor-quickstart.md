# Field Supervisor Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started

1. **Login** at `app.krewpact.com` with your MDM email
2. You'll see your **assigned projects** and today's tasks

## Your Daily Workflow

### Start of Day
1. Open your **active project** from the dashboard
2. Check **Tasks** assigned to you — update status as work progresses
3. Review any **RFIs** or **submittals** needing field input

### Daily Log Entry
1. Navigate to your project > **Daily Logs**
2. Click **New Entry** for today
3. Fill in:
   - **Weather** conditions (temperature, precipitation)
   - **Crew** count and trades on site
   - **Work performed** — describe activities completed
   - **Safety** observations or incidents
   - **Issues** or delays encountered
4. Click **Submit** to save (your PM is automatically notified)

### Safety Management
- **Safety > Toolbox Talks** — Record daily safety talks
- **Safety > Inspections** — Log site inspections
- **Safety > Incidents** — Report any safety incidents immediately
- **Safety > Forms** — Complete required safety forms

### Time Tracking
1. Navigate to project > **Time Entries**
2. Log hours for yourself and crew members
3. Select **cost code** for each entry (ties to job costing)

### Photos & Documents
- **Photos** tab — Upload site photos with annotations
- **Files** tab — Access project drawings, specs, and documents

### Deficiency Management
- **Deficiencies** tab — Log and track deficiency items
- Assign responsibility and target completion dates
- Update status as items are resolved

## Tips
- Submit daily logs **before end of day** — your PM reviews them each morning
- Photo evidence strengthens daily log entries
- Flag safety concerns immediately — they trigger notifications to management
- Use the mobile browser for on-site access

## Need Help?
Contact your Project Manager or Project Coordinator.
