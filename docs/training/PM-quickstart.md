# Project Manager Quick Start Guide

## Getting Started

1. **Login** at `app.krewpact.com` with your MDM email
2. You'll land on the **Dashboard** showing your active projects and tasks

## Your Daily Workflow

### Morning Check

- **Dashboard** shows overdue tasks, upcoming milestones, and recent activity
- **Notifications** (bell icon) flag SLA alerts and approvals needing your attention

### CRM — Managing Leads & Opportunities

1. **CRM > Leads** — View and manage incoming leads assigned to you
2. Click a lead to see details, enrichment data, and score
3. **Convert** a qualified lead: Lead → Opportunity → Estimate → Proposal → Project
4. **CRM > Opportunities** — Track active deals through pipeline stages

### Estimating

1. Navigate to **Estimates** from the sidebar
2. Click **New Estimate** to start a new bid
3. Add **line items** with quantities, unit prices, and cost codes
4. Add **allowances** for provisional sums and **alternates** for pricing options
5. Click **Save Version** to create a snapshot before sending for review
6. Once approved, generate a **Proposal** for client signature

### Project Management

1. **Projects > New Project** — Create from a signed proposal or from scratch
2. **Members** tab — Add team members (PMs, coordinators, field supervisors)
3. **Tasks** tab — Create and assign tasks with due dates
4. **Daily Logs** — Review field supervisor daily entries
5. **Files** tab — Upload and organize project documents
6. **Meetings** — Log meeting notes and decisions

### Change Management

- **Change Orders** — Create, price, and submit COs for client approval
- **RFIs** — Submit and track Requests for Information
- **Submittals** — Manage submittal packages and review cycles

## Key Shortcuts

- `Ctrl/Cmd + K` — Quick search across projects and CRM
- Click any **notification** to jump to the relevant item
- Use **filters** and **saved views** on list pages to customize your workspace

## Need Help?

Contact your Operations Manager or the Platform Admin.
